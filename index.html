OUR MANUFACTURING

AIN pursues excellence in design and irreproachable craftsmanship to create a gown worthy of a bride’s most treasured moments. We believe brides should feel nothing less than gorgeous on their wedding day. Our designs blend rich fabrics like satin and lace with decadent beading and detailing, in addition to the comfort of our patented AIN construction. The result is the AIN brand family, with collections that embody the unique style of our brides.
